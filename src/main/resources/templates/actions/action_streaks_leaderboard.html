How Streaks Work

Goal Streaks: Count consecutive match days (1-38) where a player scored at least one goal. Streak breaks when a player has a match day with 0 goals.

Assist Streaks: Count consecutive match days where a player made at least one assist. Streak breaks when a player has a match day with 0 assists.

Combined Streaks: Count consecutive match days where a player had goals OR assists (or both). Example: Player scores in MD1, assists in MD2, scores+assists in MD3 = 3-day combined streak.
